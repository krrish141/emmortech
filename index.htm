ODM Activities

IKIO Lighting Limited is a manufacturing company and an end-to-end product solutions provider in LED Lighting, Electronics, Refrigeration Lighting, Hardware, and RV Components. Catering to brands in India and abroad, we are backed by a qualified and experienced management and R&D team, consisting of members who hold a Bachelor of Technology (Electronics, Communication, and Mechanical) degree and diploma in electronics and electrical.

Our products are manufactured in our manufacturing facilities equipped with machinery and equipment like assembly lines, automatic surface-mounted machines, automatic metal cutting and shaping machines, and automatic powder coating for fixtures.
MANUFACTURING
SOLUTIONS
Production

We utilize our two decades of experience and long-term partnerships with suppliers for LEDs, electronic components, drivers, and plastics to execute production activities. We have a combined annual manufacturing capability of over 20 million pieces, for which we rely on our 4 manufacturing facilities.

Customization

Since we have manufacturing units and the resources required to handle complete product development, we can customize any product or component as per customers’ individual needs. We offer customization options, from product design to electronics to hardware and moulding.

Design

Our well-rounded product design capabilities help us fulfill specific product and customer requirements. Our R&D department focuses on product designing, tools and mould designing, electronic circuit designing, and prototype designing. Our R&D department independently develops ODM designs and verifies and develops OEM designs received from customers and converts such designs into deliverable products by improving the designs, recommending suitable raw materials, and testing of trial products.

Quality Assurance

We have implemented quality systems across our manufacturing facilities that cover the full product lifecycle from process innovation and R&D, through the stages of process development, manufacturing, sales, and supply chain, to the customer evaluation of our products as well as operation and management systems for ensuring consistent quality, efficacy and safety of our products. Our products undergo a qualification process throughout the entire life cycle from development to manufacturing.

Growth Outlook
Our Future Expansion Plans

We are in the process of expansion and growth of our current manufacturing facilities, as well as through the construction of our new manufacturing facility by our subsidiary, ISPL. This latest production unit of ours is upcoming at Noida Expressway.

We work closely with suppliers and other stakeholders to create solutions as per our clients’ needs. We aim to develop products that not only address new industry opportunities but also deliver the desired results. You can rely on us for turnkey manufacturing services and components.

Our production process is strategized, and we have the infrastructure to back it up! With an R&D lab, well-equipped assembly lines, and a team to assist, we make sure to deliver quality products. Alongside that, we also continuously audit and upgrade our technology to meet industry standards.

We follow strict quality control protocols and adhere to stringent quality requirements across end-use industries. We are certified to standards such as ISO 9001:2015, 14001:2015, 45001:2018, and OHASAS, resulting in enhanced safety for all our product buyers, employees, and end users.

We are an Indian manufacturer of light emitting diode (LED) lighting solutions and considered as a part of the Make in India initiative run by the government of India. We leverage the Indian manufacturing ecosystem for our clients, offering them a distinct competitive advantage to win in the global marketplace.

We are an Indian manufacturer of LED lighting solutions. We are focused on sustainability and providing low-energy LED products to help India meet its sustainability goals. We are primarily an original design manufacturer and design, develop, manufacture, and supply products to customers, who then further distribute these products under their own brands. We are building an international customer base, primarily in the United States.

By utilizing our four manufacturing facilities, we serve the varying needs of customers, including residential, industrial, and commercial lighting.
